Pricing Guidelines

Recommended pricing strategy:

  • Base Fare: $3-7 (covers driver time and basic costs)
  • Per KM Rate: $1.5-3.0 (based on fuel and maintenance costs)
  • Commission: 10-20% (sustainable platform operation)
  • Cancellation Fee: 25-50% (compensates drivers for time)
